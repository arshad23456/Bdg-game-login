BDG Game: Complete Guide – Login, Register, Deposit, Withdrawal & How to Play
BDG Game (Big Dream Gaming) is a popular online betting and prediction game in India where players predict outcomes to win real money. This complete guide covers everything: how to register, log in, deposit, withdraw, and play strategically.
What Is BDG Game?
BDG Game is a prediction-based betting platform where you bet on:

"Big" (numbers 6-10) or "Small" (numbers 1-5)

Colors (red, green, violet) associated with random digits

It's like a digital lottery/casino in your pocket with instant-win rewards.

Step 1: Download the app or visit the website

Android: Download BDG Game APK from the official site or Play Store

iOS: Available on the App Store

Web: Play directly from a browser

Step 2: Create your account

Open the app/website

Click "Register" or "Sign Up"

Enter your details:

Mobile number (Indian)

Email address

Choose username

Create password

Verify via SMS/Email code

Complete registration (takes <2 minutes)

Registration Requirements:

Valid Indian mobile number

Age 18+ years

Active email address

How to Login to BDG Game
Open BDG Game app or website

Click "Login" button

Enter your registered mobile/email

Enter your password

Login Tips:

If stuck, use "Forgot Password" to reset
